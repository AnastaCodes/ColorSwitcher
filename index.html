Welcome to Color Switcher!


I'm thrilled you're here. Color Switcher is my pet project crafted with the dual purpose of mastering JavaScript and simplifying the workdays of those engaged in web color selection.

Quick Guide:

Press the spacebar to generate random colors instantly. On the bottom right panel, you'll find various modules for color generation (coming soon).

Clicking the plus icon between colors blends a mid-color seamlessly between adjacent ones. You can have a maximum of 12 colors.

Color Switcher automatically updates the URL in your browser, allowing you to easily share your generated color schemes with others!

Dive in, explore, and let Color Switcher brighten your web color selection journey!

Hover over a color block to reveal control buttons:

Secure any color you desire; once locked, it won't change upon spacebar hits.
Remove unwanted colors; you must have a minimum of two colors.
Rearrange colors effortlessly through drag and drop.
Duplicate a color's code to your clipboard with a single click.
Display 20 saturation levels for the chosen color; clicking applies it to the block.
Handpick a color using your browser's standard tool.
Re-generate only the selected color, maintaining the others as they are.
